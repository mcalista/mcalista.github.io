This is my mother's recipe.

"Ice is water frozen into a solid state. Depending on the presence of impurities such as particles of soil or bubbles of air, it can appear transparent or a more or less opaque bluish-white color." read more about ice here.

What you need:
  • water in its liquid state
  • an ice tray
  • a functioning freezer
  • some time
How to use your acquired materials:
Pour the liquid water into the ice tray. Carefully move the ice tray into your freezer. Check on it now and then until it's completely solid. Time may vary with different freezer models. (Here's a tip: make your ice well in advance of when you plan on using it.) Remove your ice tray from the freezer, and remove the ice. Repeat this process to make more ice.
